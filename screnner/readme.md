npm run screener:post-process -- downloads/screen-export-…/

company_info: Contains the company name and market cap.

latest_snapshot: Contains the most recent values for CWIP, Net Block, Receivables, and Revenue.

derived_signals: Contains pre-computed forensic math:

cash_quality_5y: (5-Year CFO / 5-Year PAT).

cwip_to_net_block_ratio: (Latest CWIP / Latest Net Block). A high number indicates massive capacity expansion.

qtr_yoy_growth: YoY revenue growth for the latest quarter.

Your Task: Execute the 4-Phase Forensic Framework
Be ruthless. Discard companies that fail the math instantly. Only provide deep analysis for the final 2-3 survivors.

Phase 1 & 2 (Macro & Value-Chain Map): > Review the company_info of the list. Use your web search tools to identify the latest Indian Government policy tailwinds, budget mandates, or sector shifts relevant to them as of May 2026 (e.g., PLI schemes, defense indigenization, railway capex). Determine if the companies possess a "second-order" operating leverage advantage (e.g., supplying parts rather than fighting consumer brand wars).

Phase 3 (The Signal Audit): > Filter the list using the derived_signals:

The Death Blow: Instantly discard any company where cash_quality_5y is negative, null, or significantly below 1.0 (unless explicitly justified by an ongoing mega-capex cycle).

The Inflection Play: Isolate companies with a high cwip_to_net_block_ratio combined with a positive qtr_yoy_growth. This signals that new capacity is being built and is starting to generate revenue.

Working Capital Check: Compare latest_snapshot.receivables against latest_snapshot.revenue_latest_qtr to flag potential channel stuffing or collection risks.

Phase 3.5 (The Narrative Overlay): > For the 2-3 companies that survive the math audit, use your search tools to find qualitative data from their latest FY 2025-26 Annual Reports, Concall Transcripts, or Investor Presentations. Verify if management's narrative matches the data (e.g., are they talking about the new factory shown in the CWIP?). Flag any contingent liabilities, auditor warnings, or sudden regulatory inquiries.

Phase 4 (Technical Triggers): > Provide the exact actionable Volume Breakout and Price Resistance triggers I need to set on my trading terminal to catch the institutional markup phase for the surviving stocks. '''
