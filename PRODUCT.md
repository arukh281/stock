# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Individual trader or small team running multiple paper-trading algos against NSE cash data. They review portfolios after market close (IST), trigger EOD analyze runs, and scan positions, pending orders, and activity logs.

## Product Purpose

Hosted multi-algo paper trading sandbox: four isolated ledgers, one dashboard to monitor state and run post-close analyze. Success means trustworthy numbers, fast scanning, and zero friction when triggering or debugging a run.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, operational. Reads like a desk tool, not a consumer fintech app or marketing site.

## Anti-references

Neon crypto dashboards, navy-and-gold "finance" branding, hero metric cards, glassmorphism, gradient text, identical icon-card grids, modal-heavy workflows.

## Design Principles

1. Numbers first: typography and alignment optimized for INR and P&L scanning.
2. Restrained color: semantic green/red for P&L only; algo accents are identifiers, not decoration.
3. Familiar product patterns: tables, badges, primary CTA per card; no invented affordances.
4. Density when useful: compact labels, monospace figures, scrollable tables inside cards.
5. Honest empty states: say what is empty and why it matters.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA target. Visible focus rings, sufficient contrast on muted text, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for shimmer.
